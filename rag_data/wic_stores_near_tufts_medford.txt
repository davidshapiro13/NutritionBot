================================================================================
ABOUT WIC STORES
================================================================================

WIC (Women, Infants, and Children) is a federal nutrition program. WIC-authorized
stores accept WIC benefits (EBT card) for approved food items.

Massachusetts WIC covers:
- Fresh, frozen, and canned fruits and vegetables
- Milk, cheese, eggs
- Whole wheat bread and whole grains
- Canned fish (tuna, salmon, sardines)
- Peanut butter
- Dried or canned beans and peas
- 100% fruit juice
- Infant formula and baby food (at authorized stores)

Full supermarkets carry the full WIC range.
CVS / Walgreens carry primarily infant formula, baby food, and select staples.
Independent markets vary — call ahead to confirm specific items.
